Learn how to list and sell your AI-generated components on the CodeRocket Marketplace.

## Prerequisites

Before you can sell components, you need:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Premium Subscription">
    Only premium CodeRocket users can sell components in the marketplace
  </Step>
  <Step title="Private Components">
    Only private components can be listed for sale (not publicly visible components)
  </Step>
  <Step title="High-Quality Work">
    Ensure your components are well-designed, functional, and original
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a Listing

### 1. Access the Listing Form

From your account:
- Go to **My Listings** in the sidebar
- Click **Create Listing**
- Or from the main Marketplace page, click **Create Listing**

### 2. Select Your Component

Choose from your private components:
- Only private components appear in the dropdown
- Select the specific version you want to sell
- Preview will be generated from your component

### 3. Set Up Your Listing

Fill out the listing details:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Basic Information">
    - **Title**: Clear, descriptive name for your component
    - **Description**: Detailed explanation of what the component does
    - **Category**: Choose the most appropriate category
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pricing">
    - Set your price in USD (minimum $1.00)
    - Remember: you receive 70% after platform commission
    - Consider the complexity and value of your component
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Preview & Demo">
    - **Preview Image**: Automatically generated from your component
    - **Demo URL**: Optional link to live demo
    - High-quality previews increase sales
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### 4. Publish Your Listing

- Review all information carefully
- Click **Create Listing** to publish
- Your component becomes available for purchase immediately

## Managing Your Listings

### View Performance
Track your listings from **My Listings**:
- **Total Sales**: Number of purchases
- **Earnings**: Your share after commission
- **Views**: Listing visibility metrics

### Edit Listings
You can update:
- Title and description
- Price (affects future sales only)
- Preview image
- Demo URL

### Deactivate Listings
- Toggle listings active/inactive
- Inactive listings don't appear in marketplace
- You can reactivate anytime

## Pricing Strategy

### Recommended Pricing
- **Simple Components**: $5-15
- **Complex Components**: $15-50
- **Full Templates**: $25-100+
- **Premium/Unique**: $50-200+

### Factors to Consider
- **Complexity**: More complex = higher price
- **Uniqueness**: Rare designs command premium
- **Quality**: Professional polish increases value
- **Market**: Research similar components

## Earnings & Payouts

### Commission Structure
- **Your Share**: 70% of sale price
- **Platform Fee**: 30% for processing, hosting, support

### Payment Processing
- Payments processed via Stripe
- Earnings tracked in real-time
- Monthly payout statements available

### Tax Considerations
<Warning>
  You're responsible for tax obligations on marketplace earnings. Consult a tax professional for guidance.
</Warning>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Component Not Showing in Dropdown">
    - Ensure component is set to private
    - Check you have premium subscription
    - Component must be yours (not forked)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low Sales">
    - Improve preview image quality
    - Update description with more details
    - Consider adjusting price
    - Add demo URL if possible
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Preview Not Generating">
    - Component may have rendering issues
    - Check component works properly
    - Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
